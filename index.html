Frequently Asked Questions

What devices support Hulu TV?
Hulu TV works on smart TVs, mobile phones, tablets, laptops, gaming consoles, and streaming devices like Roku, Fire TV, and Apple TV.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes, Hulu offers flexible monthly plans. You can cancel anytime from your account settings without any cancellation fee.
Does Hulu offer a free trial?
Yes, Hulu offers a 30-day free trial for new subscribers. You can try any plan risk-free.
How many people can watch at once?
Hulu allows two simultaneous streams per account. You can upgrade to the Unlimited Screens add-on for more flexibility.
Is live TV included with all plans?
No, live TV is only available with the Hulu + Live TV plan. Other plans offer on-demand content only.
Can I download shows to watch offline?
Yes, with the Hulu (No Ads) plan or Hulu + Live TV (No Ads), you can download select content on supported mobile devices.
Are there parental controls?
Yes, Hulu allows you to set up Kids Profiles and apply content restrictions to help manage what younger viewers can access.
What channels come with Hulu + Live TV?
Hulu + Live TV includes 90+ live channels such as ABC, NBC, ESPN, FOX, CNN, and more. Channel availability may vary by location.
